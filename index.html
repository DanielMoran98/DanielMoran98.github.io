About Me

My name is Daniel Moran and I'm a final year Computing student studying Computing at Technological University Dublin. In my spare time I like to listen to podcasts, read up about the latest tech and go to the gym.

Currently I am working on my final year project which is an electronic resource manager for Án Garda Síochana. It will provide a tool to make the day to day duties of the police force more efficient.

Most of my finished and in progress projects can be viewed on my Github, and previews of them are further down on this page.
